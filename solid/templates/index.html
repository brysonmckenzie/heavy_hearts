Heavy Hearts

One unique characteristic of Heavy Hearts Movement is the unprecedented passion and support that power our initiatives is largely represented by the “Millennial Generation”, and rightfully so, considering data shows, this population is adversely impacted by gun violence more than any other. Every standing member of our organization has a driven testimony behind why it is important to make a difference in our communities. The way we go about touching and reaching our people is through experience and having the understanding that we have all been in a place of mourning and discouragement. We hope to bring hope, life, and love back into our village.
